# Short Bio #
My name is Niko, but I prefer to be called Elliot. Before completeing my BSc. of Physics in 2019, I was a lab researcher in a biological physics laboratory. Since then, I've been working as a mathematics educator for children and young adults. I view data science as the discipline of using science and technology to connect people with actionable solutions to modern problems. I'm motivated by the opportunity to build these solutions and learn from others in the process.
